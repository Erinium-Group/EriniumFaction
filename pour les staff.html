üåå Project Overview

What is Erinium Faction?

Erinium Faction is an ambitious Minecraft PvP faction server project that combines two distinct aesthetic universes: cyberpunk technology and cosmic fantasy. This unique "Cyber-Astral" theme sets us apart from traditional faction servers and creates a visually stunning, immersive experience.

The custom mods are being developed for NeoForge 1.21.1 using MCreator 2025.2+ as our primary development tool, with additional Java coding in IntelliJ IDEA for advanced features. We're building a full-featured faction warfare experience with territorial control, strategic gameplay, and epic multiplayer battles.

üéØ Core Concept

Territorial warfare meets cosmic adventure. Players form factions, claim territories infused with astral energy, wage wars using cyber-enhanced weapons, and build empires in a universe where technology and cosmic magic collide.

üîí Server-Exclusive Project: This is a custom server project. The mods we're creating are exclusive to the Erinium Faction server and will not be publicly released on CurseForge or Modrinth. This allows us to create a unique, cohesive experience that players can only find on our server.

‚öîÔ∏è Core Features

Faction System

  • Faction Creation & Management: Players can create factions with customizable names, descriptions, and Cyber-Astral banners
  • Rank System: Hierarchical structure from Recruit to Leader with customizable permissions
  • Member Management: Invite, kick, promote, and demote faction members
  • Faction Chat: Private communication channels for faction members
  • Alliances & Enemies: Diplomatic relations with other factions

Territory & Claims

  • Land Claiming: Claim chunks for your faction using astral energy resources
  • Territory Protection: Prevent enemy building/breaking in claimed territory
  • Power System: Faction power determines claim capacity and resilience
  • Territory Visualization: Cyber-themed boundary markers and HUD displays
  • Strategic Resources: Certain territories contain valuable Cyber-Astral resources

Combat & Warfare

  • Raid Mechanics: Organized faction raids on enemy territories
  • Cyber-Enhanced Weapons: Unique faction-exclusive weapons with Cyber-Astral themes
  • Siege Equipment: Craftable siege tools for breaking through defenses
  • Power Loss: Deaths reduce faction power, making territories vulnerable
  • War Declarations: Formal war system with scheduled battles

Cyber-Astral Theme Integration

  • Astral Energy: Cosmic resource used for advanced faction abilities
  • Cyber Upgrades: Technology-based enhancements for faction bases
  • Themed Blocks & Items: Neon-lit, holographic, and cosmic-themed content
  • Visual Effects: Particle effects, custom animations, and shader-friendly designs
  • Lore Integration: Deep backstory connecting cyber technology and cosmic forces

üé® Art Direction Note: Every visual element should evoke the fusion of futuristic technology (neon lights, holograms, circuits) with cosmic mysticism (stars, nebulae, ethereal energy). Think "Cyberpunk 2077 meets Interstellar."
